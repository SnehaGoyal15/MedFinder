About Us

PUNS MedFinder (Pocket Utility & No-Allergy Substitutes) is a health-tech application created to simplify medicine selection by helping users find affordable and allergy-safe alternatives.

Objective

  • Provide cost-effective substitutes for branded medicines
  • Help users avoid medications with allergens
  • Enable informed decisions using salt compositions
  • Promote use of generic medicines to cut healthcare costs

Key Features

  • Search medicines by name
  • View generic and branded alternatives with price comparisons
  • Filter suggestions based on user allergy profiles
  • Receive safe and effective medicine recommendations

Our Vision

To revolutionize medicine accessibility by providing a trusted platform that prioritizes safety, affordability, and awareness for all users.

Who We Are

We are a team of technology enthusiasts and healthcare advocates on a mission to make medicine safe and affordable for everyone, especially in underserved regions.
